Spider-Man: Across the Spiderverse

June 7, 2023 - 3:07 PM - Dolby Cinema

Family is the foundation.

Spoilers Ahead

Spider-Man: Across the Spiderverse is the ultimate kids movie. This film does not spoon-feed the details; to catch them, it encourages participation by meeting the audience half-way through its animation, which deserves to be picked apart frame-by-frame. You have to dig through and assemble the pieces to learn what a character is thinking and how they are feeling - in ways that live-action films will be hard-pressed match. This film is a great cultural service because it is teaching our future moviegoers how to watch movies.

"You have the right to remain silent."

Note: I saw this film three times in theaters. (Yes, I know that is excessive.) Watching it in a Dolby Cinema, a standard auditorium, and a Cinemark XD screen helped me understand that the way an audience watches a movie is very, very important. A Dolby Cinema with the audio turned up loud induces a similarly massive response. A standard auditorium where the dialogue is sometimes inaudible can blunt the impact of this great film. Advice for the future: where possible, please turn the volume up when watching this film at home!
