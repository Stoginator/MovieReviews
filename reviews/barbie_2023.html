Barbie

August 12, 2023 - 12:00 PM - Dolby Cinema

The meaning of womanhood... for Barbie.

We mothers stand still so our daughters can look back and see how far they’ve come.

When I saw 2017’s Lady Bird, I was immediately taken by an imitate tale of mother and daughter bound by a deep love for each other yet separated by misunderstandings. The typical type of conflicts that come between college-bound teenagers and their mothers. What struck me then was the distinct voice behind the camera: perceptively and sincerely following a flawed, determined young woman.
