June 7, 2023 - 3:07 PM - Dolby Cinema

Review Title

Spoilers Ahead

Spider-Man: Across the Spiderverse is the ultimate kids movie. This film does not spoon-feed the details; to catch them, it encourages participating in the movie by meeting the audience half-way through its animation, which deserves to be picked apart frame-by-frame. Many times, you have to dig through each shot and assemble the pieces to learn what a character is thinking and how they are feeling - in ways that live-action films cannot replicate.
